STAY SAFE ONLINE CHECKLIST

1. Do not click on unknown links or emails.
2. Always verify website URLs before entering details.
3. Never share OTP, passwords, or bank details.
4. Use strong and unique passwords.
5. Enable two-factor authentication.
6. Avoid using public Wi-Fi for transactions.
7. Regularly update your apps and software.
8. Be cautious of messages asking for urgent action.
9. Use official apps for payments and banking.
10. Report cybercrime at cybercrime.gov.in or call 1930.
